## 1. çözüm
array
[22, 27, 16, 2, 18, 6] --> n

**22** | 27 16 12 18 6 --> n-1

**22** **27** | 16 12 18 6 --> n-2

**16** **22** **27** | 12 18 6 --> n-3

**12** **16** **22** **27** | 18 6 --> n-4

**12** **16** **18** **22** **27** | 6 --> 1

**6**  **12** **16** **18** **22** **27**  --> sorted array

## 2.çözüm

T(n) = n + (n-1) + (n-2)+....+ 1 = n*(n+1)/2
Time Complexity --> O(n^2)

## 3.Çözüm

Worst Case: O(n^2)
Avarage Case: O(n^2)
Best Case: O(n)
